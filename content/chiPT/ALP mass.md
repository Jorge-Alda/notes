# $\mu_a = 0$
## $c_G = 0$
$$m_a^2 = 0$$
## $c_G \neq 0$
$$m_a^2 = \frac{2}{3}c_G^2\frac{F_\pi^2}{f_a^2}m_0^2 \left(1 - s_\eta^2 \frac{m_0^2}{m_\eta^2}-c_\eta^2\frac{m_0^2}{m_{\eta'}^2} \right) $$

# $\mu_a \neq 0$
## $c_G \neq 0$, $c_q^A = 0$
$$m_a^2 = \mu_a^2 + \frac{2}{3}c_G^2\frac{F_\pi^2}{f_a^2}m_0^2 \left(1 + \frac{m_0^2}{\mu_a^2}\right)$$
## $c_G = 0$, $c_{uu}^A \neq 0$
$$m_a^2 = \mu_a^2 + \frac{{c_{uu}^A}^2}{12} \frac{F_\pi^2}{f_a^2}\left[3 m_{\pi^0}^2 + m_\eta^2(c_\eta-\sqrt{2}s_\eta)^2+m_{\eta'}^2(\sqrt{2}c_\eta + s_\eta)^2 + \frac{3 m_{\pi^0}^2 + m_\eta^2(c_\eta-\sqrt{2}s_\eta)^2+m_{\eta'}^2(\sqrt{2}c_\eta + s_\eta)^2}{\mu_a^2}\right]$$
