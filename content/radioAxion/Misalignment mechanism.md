Non-thermal production of axions in the early Universe

When the PQ symmetry is broken, the axion field takes a value in each casual patch given by a random misalignment angle $a = \theta_i f_a$.

Once the Universe expands and $H\sim m_a(T)$, the axion field starts to oscillate coherently around the minimum of the potential.

The energy density of the axion field dilutes as $\mathfrak{a}(t)^{-3}$, thus it constitutes cold DM.

$$\theta(t) = \theta_0 \cos(m_a t + \varphi)\,,\qquad \theta_0 = \frac{\sqrt{2\rho_\mathrm{DM}}}{m_a f_a}$$
