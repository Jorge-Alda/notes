Pion mass $$m_\pi^2(\theta) = m_\pi^2(0) \cos \frac{\theta}{2}\sqrt{1 +\frac{(m_d-m_u)^2}{(m_d+m_u)^2}\tan^2\frac{\theta}{2}}$$

Nucleon mass difference $\Longrightarrow$ $\beta$-decays and E.C. $$\Delta m_N(\theta) = m_n(\theta)-m_p(\theta) = -4 c_5 \frac{m_d-m_u}{m_d+m_u}\frac{m_\pi^4(0)}{m_\pi^2(\theta)}$$

Nucleon contact interactions $$-\mathcal{L}\supset G_S\, \eta_S(\theta) (\bar N N)(\bar N N)\,,\qquad\qquad \eta_S(\theta) = 1.4 -0.4\frac{m_\pi^2(\theta)}{m_\pi^2(0)}$$

Nuclear binding energy $\Longrightarrow$ $\alpha$-decays $$\delta\mathrm{BE}(\theta) = (120A-97A^{2/3})(\eta_S(\theta)-1)\,\mathrm{MeV}$$
